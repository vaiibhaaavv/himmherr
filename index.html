Founder & Creative Director

Vaibhav Sharma

Hello, I’m Vaibhav Sharma.

Born and raised in Delhi, I’ve always found beauty in simplicity — in fabrics that soften with time, in colors that calm the eye, and in clothing that becomes part of your story rather than defining it.

After spending years in the fashion industry, I realised what I truly wanted to create: pieces that don’t chase trends, but instead offer a sense of lasting ease and belonging.

In 2025, I founded himm and herr in London while I was a student in the UK with the belief that everyday clothing can still be thoughtful, sustainable, and beautiful. Every collection is designed in our Delhi studio with premium natural fabrics and a deep respect for quiet elegance.
